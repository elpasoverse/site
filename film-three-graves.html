Three Graves

Ryan Wiik | 18 min | Spain | 2026 | Short

A tale of vengeance and redemption

In the unforgiving borderlands of 1880, a lone wanderer discovers three freshly dug graves at the edge of a forgotten settlement. Each grave marks a story of betrayal, each stone a promise unkept.

As dust storms roll across the desert and shadows lengthen over sun-bleached wood, the frontier reveals its truths slowly—through weathered faces, loaded guns, and the weight of choices that echo across the wasteland.

Shot entirely on Kodak motion picture film stock, Three Graves captures the raw beauty and brutal reality of the Old West in its perpetual twilight year of 1880.
