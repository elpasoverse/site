Three Graves

Revenge Western | 18 min | Spain | 2026 | Short

A tale of cold blooded vengeance and betrayal

Three Graves is a visceral, atmospheric short film set in the unforgiving borderland beyond Ciudad Juarez. In the era before the region was claimed by settlers, the frontier exists in a state of perpetual twilight—a lawless expanse where the land itself acts as both judge and executioner.

Shot entirely on 16mm motion picture film stock, the film prioritizes a raw, tactile aesthetic.

Limited Edition

1-of-1 Motion Prints

Every frame is a painting captured on authentic Kodak film stock. A limited number of 1-of-1 frames from the original print—each unique, numbered, and tradeable.
