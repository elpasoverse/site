The Visionary

Adventure Western | 100 min | Spain | 2026 | Feature Film

You don't find it. You follow the pull.

A man follows a pull he cannot name and vanishes into the desert. What he finds there rewrites everything he thought he was chasing — and everything chasing him.

An epic journey into the heart of the El Paso Verse, where Mystery, Adventure, Drama, and the Western frontier collide in a feature-length cinematic experience.
