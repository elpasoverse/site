Overview

A historic monastery-turned-hotel nestled in the Sierra Alhamilla region of Almeria, Spain. The property offers immediate operational infrastructure with a 21-room boutique hotel, restaurant, terrace bar, and spa facilities — all set against a dramatic mountain backdrop.

The site includes a natural hot spring with wellness potential, gardens suitable for camping expansion, and a shell structure for 43 balcony apartments ready for Phase II completion. Located just a 15-minute drive from existing Western film sets in the Tabernas region, this property serves as an immediate operational base — a foundation for production, event hosting, and immersive frontier hospitality.
