Sombra de Lobo

The shadow of the wolf

1880 • El Paso Verse Shot on Kodak Motion Picture Film Limited Edition Collectibles

They call him Sombra de Lobo—the shadow of the wolf. A figure who moves through the borderlands like smoke, leaving no trace but the stories whispered in cantinas and around campfires.

In the harsh light of 1880, where the line between Mexico and the territories blurs in the desert heat, one man's legend becomes another's nightmare. Born from violence, shaped by the unforgiving land, he is both predator and protector—a ghost of the Old West that refuses to die.

Captured on authentic Kodak film stock, Sombra de Lobo is a haunting portrait of survival, identity, and the price of myth in a world that devours men whole.
