A VISION FOR THE FRONTIER

An Unprecedented Destination Where the Old West Lives in Perpetuity

By Harrison West, Founder & Trailblazer

EL PASO VERSE was born in my imagination as an escape from modern day reality as I sought to capture profound cinematic moments near the U.S. and Mexico borderlands of the 1880s.

While it has been possible to recreate these moments on film, there exists a deeper yearning to discover something more... What if there was a place—one hundred percent authentic to that time period—where you could not only experience it on the silver screen but walk the land yourself, feeling the elements with all your senses and live your story as if it would have been in the year 1880?

✦
"A land so vast that traveling across it on horseback would take days in any direction"
✦

There is a way to deliver this adventure to those who seek it. And I believe there is no one who wouldn't want to step into this world, if only for a moment... For those ready to immerse themselves in a longer, more profound experience, we invite you to become part of the El Paso Verse—a world where you can spend years living the adventure.

A land so vast that traveling across it on horseback would take days in any direction, or you could choose to hop aboard an interconnected steam train to visit distant towns. It is a place where you can choose to thrive as an entrepreneur, build a business, perhaps fall in love, make meaningful friendships or simply explore as a man or woman passing through it.

The vision for El Paso Verse is multi-faceted, and over the coming years, it needs to be developed collectively as a community.

This document serves as an overview for the ecosystem that will power and propel this vision into reality — a vision that I believe soon will become one of the most sought-after destinations on the planet.

— H. West

NOT A METAVERSE.
IT'S A PLACE.

Most Web3 projects want to digitize the world. We want to experience what it was like to live in one of the most iconic frontiers in human history.

El Paso Verse is a real-world destination—built from the ground up—where story, technology, and land meet in a new way. A community-driven universe where the Old West is reborn in the perpetual year of 1880.

It begins with cinema. It ends with community.

A PHYSICAL DESTINATION

El Paso Verse will take members of the public into the 1880s for real. Not virtual reality—actual reality. A first-of-its-kind global destination built on fifty thousand plus acres of authentic Western territory.

The Hub serves as your portal to 1880, with multiple frontier towns at different levels—from daily visitors experiencing storylines and entertainment, to long-term residents and ranch owners who call the frontier home.

Experience live shows, participate in storylines, own businesses, or become a homesteader. This unprecedented destination brings the frontier to life through immersive theater, community governance, and real land ownership.
