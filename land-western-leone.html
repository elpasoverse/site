Overview

Originally constructed for Sergio Leone's Once Upon a Time in the West, this property sits in Europe's only true desert — the Tabernas Desert of Almeria, Spain — offering a year-round dry climate ideal for Western film production.

The site retains authentic Western town structures from the original production. Located approximately a two-hour horseback ride from Fort Bravo Texas Hollywood, the property has potential for regional expansion, though intervening land is controlled by multiple private landowners.
